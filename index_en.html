Jingwei Qu (瞿经纬)

Short Bio
Jingwei Qu received B.S. from the School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science, Peking University, Beijing, China, in 2014. He is currently a Ph.D. candidate in Wangxuan Institute of Computer Technology, Peking University. He has been a joint Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer and Information Sciences, Temple University, from 2018 to 2019. His research interests include graph neural networks, computer vision, and molecule analysis.
